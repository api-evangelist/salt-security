Salt Security argues that cybersecurity startups have been optimized to win proof-of-value evaluations rather than to actually solve customer problems. As agents connect to APIs, attackers can use them to explore and exploit weak authorization paths faster, underscoring the gap between POV-driven products and real-world security outcomes.
